About Me

My name is Liv Pedersen. I am a student at Brigham Young University and I am currently applying for the Experience Design and Management program in the Marriot School of Business. I am passionate about people, leadership, service, and truly making a difference in the lives of others. I've come to find that connection is the most important thing to cultivate when it comes to working with others in order to find success.

Outside of school and work, I enjoy living, laughing, occasionally loving, and long walks on any beach. I'm always looking for opportunities to grow professionally and personally, so naturally I am always down for any adventure. From filling up hot air balloons at 6am to driving to hiking literally anywhere to watch the sunset, anytime is good time when it's with the people you love.
